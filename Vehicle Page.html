THE VEHICLES IN PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUND

  1. UAZ Open Top- The UAZ is a medium torque car that can hold up to 5 people. Has low acceleration but lots of armor.
  2. UAZ Closed Top- The UAZ is a medium torque car that can hold up to 5 people. Has low acceleration but even more armor than a open top.
  3. Dacia- The Dacia is a low torque car that can hold up to 5 people as well. Has low acceleration and medium armor.
  4. Motorcycle with Sidecar- The Motorcycle with sidecar is a medium torque vehicle that can hold up to 3 people. Had high acceleration but low armor. Land vehicle
  5. Motorcycle- The Motorcycle is a high torque vehicle than can hold 2 people. Has high acceleration and low armor. Land vehicle
  6. PG-117- The PBG-117 is a boat with medium acceleration and torque that can hold up to 5 people. It has high armor and is a water vehicle
  7. Buggy- The Buggy is a medium torque car with high acceleration. It can hold up to 2 people and is a land vehicle.
